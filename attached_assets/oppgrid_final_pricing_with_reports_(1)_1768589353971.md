## Executive Summary

### Three-Gate Revenue Model

**Gate 1: Platform Access (Subscription Required)**
- Pay to browse and see opportunities
- No free tier ‚Üí zero value extraction
- Individual: $20-99/month | Business: $250-2,500+/month

**Gate 2: Opportunity Slots (Limited Exclusivity)**
- Included slots based on tier (1-30/month)
- Additional slots: $20-50 each
- Only 3-10 people can access each opportunity

**Gate 3: Execution Reports (20 AI-Generated Reports)**
- Available only after claiming a slot
- Individual pricing: $49-$189 per report
- Business pricing: $79-$319 (white-label included)
- Bundles save 20-30%

### Why This Works

‚úÖ **$20 Entry Point** - 10x more accessible than competitors  
‚úÖ **Gated Platform** - Must pay to see anything (no free browsing)  
‚úÖ **Real Exclusivity** - Limited slots per opportunity (3-10 max)  
‚úÖ **Comprehensive Execution** - 20 reports cover every launch need  
‚úÖ **Natural Upgrades** - Individual ‚Üí Business as projects scale
